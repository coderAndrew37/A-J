Our Mission

At Embu Pork Suppliers, our mission is to provide fresh, high-quality pork cuts that meet the needs of small butcheries. We focus on timely deliveries, exceptional service, and building long-lasting partnerships with our customers.

Our Story

Embu Pork Suppliers started with a vision to connect small butcheries in Embu County with premium pork products. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a trusted name known for quality, consistency, and reliability.
